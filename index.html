About

I am a Systems and Technology Librarian at Bridgewater State University, where I manage core library systems, lead the redesign of the library website, and implement technology solutions that enhance discovery, access, and user experience. Previously, I served as a Digital Scholarship Librarian specializing in data science, data visualization, and research data managemen. With master’s degrees in Library and Information Science and Information Design and Technology, I leverage technical expertise to advance digital library initiatives and build data literacy. I invite you to explore my work and connect with me around shared interests in digital scholarship, data science, and library technology.
